About Me

Tako Natsvlishvili is a Georgian model who was born on 9th October, 1998. Born and raised in Georgian capital Tbilisi, Tako pursued her modeling career all the way in New York City. Known for her association with Giorgio Armani, Tako is a name that everyone knows well.
